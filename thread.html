OxygenOS 13.1.0.602 for the OnePlus Pad

Aug 07, 15:28 Last edit: Aug 07, 15:27

125 65 65

Hello everyone,

We have pushed an incremental rollout of OxygenOS 13.1.0.602 for the OnePlus Pad. The initial rollout has finished already and the update has reached most devices by now.

OnePlus Pad

OPD2203_13.1.0.602(EX01)

Changelog:

This update improves system stability and security and adds support for Spatial Audio.

  • System:

  • Integrates the July 2023 Android security patch to enhance system security.

  • Adds support for Spatial Audio to improve the audio experience when using headphones.

  • Improves the user experience of Notes and Smart Sidebar.

  • Improves system stability.

As always, this OTA will be incremental. The OTA will reach a small percentage of users today, and we'll begin a broader rollout in a few days.


Your feedback is appreciated, and we invite you to share it below. The OnePlus Pad is the first product of an entirely new line, so your comments are even more important. We may not always be able to respond to every message in the Community, but please be assured that we take your comments and feedback seriously.

We hope you enjoy the new build and let us know how you feel about the update in the comments below.

Never Settle.
